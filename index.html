About the author

The voice behind the book.

Frank Raj is a poet, columnist, inspirational speaker, and author with a Master’s degree in Professional Creative Writing from Falmouth University. He was also the founding editor and publisher of The International Indian, a diaspora magazine based in Dubai.

His writing reflects a lifelong engagement with faith, reflection, poetry, and the inner search for spiritual meaning.
